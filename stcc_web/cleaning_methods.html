Overview

We use only the large truck mount extractors, or commonly called "truck mounts." This is the number one method recommended by the companies that make the carpet in the first place. It is the method used by most professional carpet cleaners. The reason not every carpet cleaner will use this method is because it cost way more money than any other machine. I mean thousands more.

A person could buy any machine under $2,000.00 to $3,000.00. Buy a truck mount, and I mean one of the big ones, the small ones don't work properly. These will cost over 20,000 dollars for a good one with no trimmings just the big turkey with out any of the dressing.

We follow our method for each job. It takes longer to do these steps, but all steps are necessary:

1

We give an exact quote.

2

We prep the job by moving things off the floor -- such as shoes, books, and so on.

3

We pre-treat the carpet with a pre-spotter for the bad areas. We have a few different spot removers for various spots.

We can even remove red dye from different drinks and juices. Removing red dye may be an additional cost but we can teach you how to do it and give you a toll free number to order it yourself and save money.

4

A) We then will move the furniture away from the wall.

B) We then will put the specially designed tools on for cleaning edges and along the baseboards and clean that first. This takes time but the end result is perfect. This is why we say "We clean the corners that others cut"®

C) Then we clean behind the furniture.

D) Then we put the furniture back and place the carpet and furniture protectors under the furniture legs where needed.

E) We then clean the rest of the room.

F) Finally we will brush or groom the carpet.
